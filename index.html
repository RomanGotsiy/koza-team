We are a group of artists working together to bring our best ideas to life.
Our goal is to create uniquely beautiful high quality videos, photos, and social projects.
Through the creation of our projects we want to popularize low-budget filmmaking and photography among ukrainians.
Our aim is to be recognized not only in Ukraine, but all around the world.
